About Udbhav

Udbhav ’19 is an electrifying fiesta wherein colleges all across Delhi NCR will combat in a 2-day extravaganza to overcome their fears, to win hearts and to prove their mettle. It’s a perfect blend of Technical, Cultural, Literary, Fun, and many more events. It has a promise of being vibrant & enthusiastic, having a legacy of large participation & feverish thrill.
